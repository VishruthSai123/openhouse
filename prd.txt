Product Requirements Document (PRD)

Product: Open House – Student Founder Platform
Owner: Manjunath Meesala
Date: 17-Oct-2025

1. Purpose & Vision

Purpose:
Open House is a social-first platform for students and young creators to connect, collaborate, and launch startups. It solves the common problems student founders face: finding co-founders, accessing mentorship, building ideas into products, and discovering early-stage funding.

Vision:
“To create a global ecosystem where student founders and creators of all roles (idea generators, developers, designers, editors, marketers) can build real startups from dorm rooms to global scale.”

2. Goals & Objectives

Build a community-focused platform that encourages collaboration and mentorship.

Enable students to join in any role (founder, developer, designer, editor, marketer, mentor).

Provide tools to build startups: Idea Hub, Build Spaces, Pitch Deck templates, AI assistance.

Introduce gamification & rewards (Builder Coins, Idea Battles, Seasons).

Provide investor visibility & mentorship access.

Maintain low entry barrier (₹100 entry fee) while keeping exclusivity.

3. Target Audience

College students (all years) interested in entrepreneurship

Young creators with skills: coding, designing, editing, marketing

Mentors & investors looking to discover early-stage talent

Startups in ideation or early development stage

4. User Roles & Permissions
Role	Capabilities	Access
Idea Creator / Founder	Post ideas, find team, participate in Idea Battles, track startup progress	Full
Developer	Join projects, collaborate in Build Spaces, contribute to MVPs	Project-based
Designer	Join projects, contribute UI/UX & branding	Project-based
Editor / Content Creator	Join projects, create marketing content, promote ideas	Project-based
Marketer / Growth	Join projects, run campaigns, track metrics	Project-based
Mentor	Browse ideas/teams, mentor 1:1 or speed mentoring, provide feedback	Full (read/write limited)
Investor	Browse startups & teams, book meetings, fund startups	Limited (read-only for public data)
5. Core Features & Requirements
5.1 Founder & Creator Profiles

Showcase skills, roles, achievements, and projects

Display startup journey, milestones & Builder Coins

Public/Private visibility settings

5.2 Idea Hub

Post and share startup ideas

AI-powered idea validation & suggestions

Commenting, voting, and collaboration features

5.3 Co-Founder & Team Match

AI-powered matchmaking based on skills, goals, roles, and interests

Notifications for matches & team invites

5.4 Build Spaces

Private workspaces per team/project

Features: chat, task boards, roadmap builder, pitch deck templates

Role-based task assignments & notifications

5.5 Mentorship & Investor Access

Mentor roulette for random weekly speed mentoring

Book 1:1 sessions

Verified investors can browse startups

5.6 Gamification / Builder Coins

Earn coins for contributions: coding, designing, editing, pitching, mentoring

Leaderboards & unlockable perks (mentorship, tools, investor access)

5.7 Founder Feed

Distraction-free feed for team updates, achievements, launches

Ability to share posts, media, and track interactions

5.8 Idea Battle Arena

Pitch ideas in short videos

Community voting & AI scoring

Top ideas highlighted to investors

5.9 Startup Journey Tracker

Track milestones: Idea → MVP → Launch → Growth

Progress visualization for team & investors

5.10 Founder Stage / Live Rooms

Live audio/video pitch sessions

AMA, hackathons, and live collaboration

Investor Q&A sessions

5.11 Startup Passport

Digital resume for students: skills, roles, projects, achievements, badges

Shareable externally

5.12 Payment & Entry Fee

One-time entry fee: ₹100

Payment gateway integration (UPI, cards, wallets)

Optional Open House Pro upgrade

6. User Flow

Step 1: Sign up → Select role → Build profile → Pay ₹100
Step 2: Post ideas or join projects → Match with teammates
Step 3: Work in Build Spaces → Track progress → Earn Builder Coins
Step 4: Participate in Idea Battles, mentor sessions, live events
Step 5: Pitch to investors → Launch startup → Add milestones to Startup Passport

7. Technical Requirements

Platform: Web-first, with mobile app (iOS & Android)

Backend: Scalable cloud-based solution (Firebase / Node.js / PostgreSQL)

AI Features:

Co-Founder match AI

Idea validation & pitch suggestions

Real-time Collaboration: Chat, task boards, live rooms

Gamification Engine: Builder Coins, Leaderboards, Rewards

Payment Integration: Razorpay / Stripe / UPI wallets

8. KPIs / Success Metrics

Student signups (target: 10k in first 6 months)

Active projects & collaborations

Number of idea pitches & battles submitted

Mentorship sessions booked & completed

Startups launched within the platform

Engagement: posts, votes, Builder Coins earned

9. Roadmap (MVP → Full Launch)

Phase 1 (MVP)

Founder profiles, Idea Hub, Co-Founder Match, Build Spaces, Entry Fee integration

Phase 2

Mentorship & Investor Access, Gamification, Builder Coins

Phase 3

Idea Battle Arena, Startup Journey Tracker, Founder Stage Live Rooms

Phase 4 (Scale)

Startup Passport, AI Co-Founder Assistant, Open House Seasons, global expansion

10. Risks & Mitigation
Risk	Mitigation
Low student adoption	Campus ambassador programs, viral campaigns
Lack of mentor availability	Mentor incentives, AI guidance
Payment failures	Multiple gateways, UPI + card + wallet support
Early-stage quality control	Verified mentors, AI-assisted idea validation
